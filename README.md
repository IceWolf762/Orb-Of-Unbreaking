This mod adds a new item, the Orb of Unbreaking!

## What does it do?
When used, it will make whatever Netherite-tier weapon or tool is in your Off-Hand slot to become Unbreakable. But there requirements...

## Crafting Recipe
1 Nether Star
4 Ender Pearls
4 Netherite Ingots

Arrange as shown...

![Orb of Unbreaking Crafting Recipe](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/d4027211178fd687597f6ebd35fd671c39198751.png)

**THE WEAPON/TOOL MUST ALSO HAVE THE __MENDING__ AND __UNBREAKING__ ENCHANTS (any level).**

The Orb will be visible under the "Tools & Utilities" tab in the Creative Inventory.

## Using the Orb
Once you have the Orb, place the target tool/weapon in to your Off-Hand slot and then "Use" the Orb and TA-DA! It will now never break!

## Supported Tools/Weapons
Netherite Pickaxe/Axe/Shovel/Hoe/Sword
